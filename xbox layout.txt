2016 Xbox Layout

left Stick - Arcade Drive
Right Stick - Tank Drive

Arm Controls
A - Upper Arm Back
B - Lower Arm Forward
X - Lower Arm Back
Y - Upper Arm Forward

Left Trigger - Take Picture (Arm)
Right Trigger - Activate Trigger (shoot)

Push Right Stick - Toggle shooter (on/off)

Back - Intake

Left Bumper - Toggle Intake Piston
Right Bumper - Toggle Angle
